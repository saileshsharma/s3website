Reasons to Signup

  • Stay in control

    If you lose your card, freeze it in the app and get a new one. (Or defrost it if you find it again.)

  • Rest easy

    Money in your Monzo account is protected by FDIC, you’re insured up to $250,000.

  • Take out cash

    We won’t charge you to withdraw cash in the US, but you may be charged by the ATM operator. When you’re abroad, the first $200 every 30 days are free, with a 3% charge after that.

  • Spend for free

    We don’t charge fees for spending on your card, we don’t charge overdraft fees, and we don’t mark up the exchange rate when you go abroad.
