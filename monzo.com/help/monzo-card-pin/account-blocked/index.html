My account is blocked

If you put the wrong PIN in too many times when making a transfer or changing your account settings, well block it to keep your account safe.

__To unblock it, head to your "Account" tab, then tap on numbers. __

Youll need your card with you, to put in the 3-digit CVC code on the back. Follow the instructions, and well unblock your PIN it usually takes less than 10 minutes
